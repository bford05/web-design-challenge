Summary: Latitude vs. Wind Speed

The wind speed of global cities seem to range mostly from 0 to 25 mph with the exception of a few outliers. Regardless of where the cities are located geographically, the wind speeds measured are consistent.

There could have been some sporadic changes in weather that resulted in the outliers on the scatter plot. Cities that are further away from the equator recorded the outliers. From the maximum temperature plot, we know that cities further away from the equator have cooler weather. For those same cities, one can infer that the wind speed could be higher but more analysis would be required to confirm this theory.
