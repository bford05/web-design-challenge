Summary: Latitude vs. Humidity

The humidity percentage of various cities around the world was assessed against the latitude coordinates. Most humidity percentages are less or equal to 100 regardless of whether a particular city is closer or further away from the equator.

In some instances, cities closer to the equator recorded low humidity percentages and recorded high humidity percentages in other instances. Since the recordings were taken in late May from global cities, the range in humidity percentages could be attributed to the seasonal differences in those areas (i.e. spring season in some cities versus summer seasons in other cities).
