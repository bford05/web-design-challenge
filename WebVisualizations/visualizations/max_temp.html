Summary: Latitude vs. Max Temp

When analyzing the maximum temperatures of various cities across the world against their latitude coordinates, we discovered that the maximum temperatures are significantly higher in cities with latitude values closer to zero. Cities with latitude values closer to zero are geographically closer to the equator which explains the increase in maximum temperatures.

Cities with latitude values further away from 0 recorded much cooler temperatures and are geographically located further away from the equator. The data aligns completely to expectations and scientific facts.
