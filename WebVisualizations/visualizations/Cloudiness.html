Summary: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

The percentage of cloudiness was evaluated against the latitude of global cities. Based on the scatter plot, there is no clear relationship between the variables. Regardless of the location of the city, there is wide range of cloudiness levels. At any given instant of time, the cloudiness of any given city could be relatively low or high.

There could be other factors not displayed in the plot that could contribute to overall cloudiness of a city. Further research and analysis would be required.
